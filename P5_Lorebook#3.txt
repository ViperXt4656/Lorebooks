The Phantom Thieves of Hearts is a group of Persona‑users based in Tokyo who change the hearts of corrupt adults by infiltrating the Metaverse, a cognitive realm from humanity's collective unconscious, and stealing the "Treasure" – the physical manifestation of distorted desires.

Palace is a unique Metaverse structure tied to an individual's strongly distorted desires. Only those with especially strong distortions have a Palace. Stealing the Palace's Treasure causes the Palace to collapse and the owner in the real world to experience a change of heart, confessing their crimes.

Metaverse is an alternate, distorted version of reality within humanity's collective unconscious. The Phantom Thieves operate inside the Metaverse to change hearts, fight Shadows, and steal Treasures. It can be accessed via the Metaverse Navigator app.

Mementos is the Palace of the general public – a vast, ever‑changing labyrinth accessible from Shibuya. It represents society's collective distorted desires and apathy. Unlike individual Palaces, Mementos has no single ruler and is used to complete requests and grind for experience.

To enter a Palace, three keywords are required: the target's full name, the type of their distortion, and the location where the distortion is anchored. To enter Mementos, only the single keyword "Mementos" is needed. The Metaverse Navigator app uses these keywords to open the portal.

Calling Card is a notice sent to a Palace target before the final infiltration of their Palace. Sending a calling card makes the Treasure tangible and vulnerable in the Metaverse. Without a calling card, the Treasure cannot be stolen and the change of heart will not occur.

Persona is the manifestation of a person's inner psyche – a "mask" to face life's hardships. In the Metaverse, a Persona takes the form of a mythological or historical figure and is used to fight Shadows. Only those who have accepted their true self can summon a Persona.

Elemental attributes in Persona combat are ten types: Physical, Gun, Fire, Ice, Electric, Wind, Psychokinesis (Psy), Nuclear (Nuke), Bless (Light/Holy), and Curse (Dark/Eiha). Each Persona and Shadow has unique affinities to these elements.

Weakness, resistance, null, repel, drain are elemental affinities. Weakness takes double damage and grants the attacker an extra "1 More" turn. Resistance reduces damage. Nullification negates all damage. Repel reflects damage back. Drain absorbs damage as HP.

Technical Damage is a battle mechanic that deals extra damage when a character hits an enemy with a specific element while the enemy is afflicted with a status ailment (Burn, Freeze, Shock, Dizzy, Forget, Sleep, Rage, Fear, Despair). Technical damage works regardless of the enemy's natural affinities.

Metaverse Navigator is a phone application that allows Persona‑users to enter the Metaverse. It requires three keywords for individual Palaces or simply "Mementos" for the collective Palace. The app was originally created from cognitive psience research.

Cognitive Psience is the study of the Metaverse and how cognition shapes reality. It was pioneered by Wakaba Isshiki (Futaba's mother) and later continued fraudulently by Ichiryusai Madarame. It explains why distorted desires create Palaces and why changes of heart work.
